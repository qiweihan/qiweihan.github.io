Marine biodiversity monitoring requires scalability and reliability across complex underwater environments. This work develops a **Unified Information Pipeline** that standardises heterogeneous datasets for cross-domain evaluation, identifies *"Context Collapse"* as a key failure mode in sparse scenes, and validates low-cost edge hardware for remote invasive species monitoring in Arctic and Atlantic ecosystems.
