I am an Assistant Professor of Business Analytics (tenure-track) at [Nova School of Business and Economics](https://www.novasbe.unl.pt/), where I am also an Affiliated Researcher at the Data, Operation & Technology Knowledge Center. My research lies at the intersection of econometrics, machine learning, and computational social science, with applications in healthcare, tourism, digital marketing, and online platforms.

## Research Interests

- Econometrics and causal inference with machine learning methods
- Computational social science and consumer behavior
- Digital health and healthcare informatics
- Online platforms, social media, and e-commerce
- ICT policy and the economics of digitization
