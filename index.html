I am a Research Scientist in Nova School of Business and Economicss and Data Science for Social Good Europe in collaboration with Center for Data Science and Public Policy at University of Chicago. Before that, I received Ph.D. in Engineering and Public Policy and M.S. in Information Networking from Carnegie Mellon University.

My research is at the intersection of econometrics and machine learning, using complex data-driven approaches in areas including

  • Applied data science in social good, such as healthcare, education, tourism
  • Consumer behavior in both online and offline channel
  • ICT management and policy
